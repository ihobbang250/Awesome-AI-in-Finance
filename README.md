> Welcome to **Awesome-AI-in-Finance**, a curated collection of high-quality papers exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Finance. This repository highlights groundbreaking research in Financial AI while also introducing potential AI Idea with the capacity to transform finance. Contributions are highly encouraged—feel free to suggest additions or improvements to enrich this growing resource!
